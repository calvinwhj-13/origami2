The Crane

this is a step by step process to making a crane

fold the paper diagonally

fold in half

fold one pocket toward other side

press the top while it is standing

flattten the pocket such that it becomes square and repeat process on other side

Fold two edges in, to form a kite-shape on top

repeat the step again on the other side

crease outer edge inside

fold one legs outwards

fold other leg outwards aswell

fold tip of one leg inwards

fold both wings outwards

voila!you have made a finished crane
